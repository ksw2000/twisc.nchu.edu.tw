Introduction

About TWISC@NCHU

The Taiwan Information Security Center (TWISC) was established on April 1, 2006. It mainly integrates the resources of domestic universities and research institutions to strengthen the research and development of National Information Security.

TWISC@NCHU is a sub-center of TWISC. Since January 1, 2015, it has participated in the deep cultivation of fundamental industrial technology projects funded by the Ministry of Science and Technology. The rise of the Internet of Things has attracted much attention from the industry, commerce and academia, and maintaining the security between its endpoints has become a challenging research topic. The short-term goal of TWISC@NCHU is to develop the foundation of key technologies for information security. The preliminary plan focuses on the development of next-generation smart meters, the information security detection and protection mechanism of renewable energy equipment and systems, and the interactive dense network and intrusion detection system for key infrastructure, then gradually expand to other critical information infrastructure. The medium and long-term goal is to develop forward-looking information security technology. Currently, the main research topics are "PUFs in the AIoT Age: Design Methods and Applications" and "A Proposal of Active Reconnaissance and Defense System in Smart Manufacturing".

TWISC@NCHU was established in line with the development of the national "Information Security is National Security" strategy, and to strengthen the teaching, research, promotion and service needs of information security.

The tasks are as follows:

  1. Strengthen information security academic and R&D capabilities.
  2. Promote industry-university research cooperation on information security.
  3. Promote international cooperation and exchange on information security.
  4. Cultivation and education promotion of information security talents.

關於中心

中心簡介

資通安全研究與教學中心(Taiwan Information Security Center，簡稱TWISC)於2006年4月1日成立，主要整合國內各大學及研究機構的資源，以強化國家資通安全之研究與發展。

本中心為TWISC中部的子中心(TWISC@NCHU)，於2015年1月1日起參與科技部所資助之深耕工業基礎技術專案計畫，於2017年3月21日通過校內研發會議正式成立。物聯網的興起備受工商業及學術界矚目，保持其終端間的安全性，已是具有挑戰的研究議題。本中心近程目標為研發資安關鍵技術基礎，前期計畫著重在下一世代智慧電表的開發、再生能源設備與系統的資安檢測與防護機制與關鍵基礎設施互動密網與入侵偵測系統，然後逐漸擴展到其他關鍵資訊基礎設施。中、長程目標為研發前瞻資安科技技術，目前主要研究議題為「人工智慧時代的硬體安全的威脅與防護」與「智慧製造的主動式偵測與防禦系統」。

本中心為配合國家「資安即國安」政策思維發展，加強資通安全之教學、研究、推廣與服務需求而成立。

中心任務如下：

  1. 加強資通安全學術與研發能量。
  2. 促進資通安全產學研合作。
  3. 促進資通安全國際合作與交流。
  4. 資通安全人才之培育與教育推廣。
